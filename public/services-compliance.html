The Evolution of the "Green Card"

Clarifying the shift from CSOC to AWSHCS.

What is AWSHCS?

Formerly known as the Construction Safety Orientation Course (CSOC) or colloquially as the "Green Card", the Apply Workplace Safety and Health in Construction Sites (AWSHCS) is the mandatory safety passport for all construction workers.

  • Mandate: Must pass within 2 weeks of arrival.
  • Curriculum: Work at Height, Fire Safety, Excavation, PTW systems.
  • Recertification: Every 2 years (or 4 years for veterans >6 yrs exp).

RNS handles course registration, exam scheduling, and card collection, ensuring your workforce remains site-compliant without administrative downtime.

Workforce Upgrading

4 Pathways to Higher-Skilled (R1)

Achieve the 10% R1 threshold and unlock significant levy savings.

🏆

1. CoreTrade Scheme

Best For: Specialists deeply skilled in a single trade.

Requires 4 years experience (Tradesman) or 6 years (Foreman). Covers key trades like Reinforced Concrete, Structural Steel, and Electrical Wiring.

↔️

2. Multi-Skilling Scheme

Best For: Versatile general workers.

Recognizes workers competent in two different trades (e.g., Tiling and Plastering) or one trade plus a safety certification.

💰

3. Direct R1 Pathway

Best For: High-quality new hires.

For candidates with a fixed monthly salary >$1,600 and passing the SEC(K) test at a higher skill level. Allows immediate R1 entry.

🥇

4. Market-Based Framework (MBF)

Best For: Experienced veterans (>6 years) earning >$1,600 who may not fit specific testing criteria but have proven market value.

Definitions

Key Trade Competencies

Reinforced Concrete

Fabrication and installation of steel rebar and formwork for structural skeletons.

Structural Steel

Cutting, welding, fitting, and erection of steel beams and columns.

Tiling & Stone

Precise cutting and laying of ceramic, marble, or granite for aesthetic finishing.

Plumbing

Installation of water/gas pipes and pressure testing for leak-free operations.

Electrical Wiring

Installation of conduit, trunking, cables, and switchboards with safety protocols.

General Worker

Essential site logistics, housekeeping, and support for skilled tradesmen.
