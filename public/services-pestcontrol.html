Scientific Approach

Vector Control Workflow

Our technicians follow a biological workflow to ensure effective and safe exclusion of pests, prioritizing non-chemical methods where possible.

1. Inspection & ID

Identifying the specific vector (Aedes, Termite Mud Tubes) and breeding sources.

2. Analysis & Plan

Deciding between chemical (fogging/misting) or non-chemical (traps/exclusion) methods.

3. Treatment

Safe application of pesticides by NEA-licensed vectors using full PPE.

4. NEA Reporting

Mandatory submission of vector control reports to ensure regulatory compliance.
