Who We Are

Building Singapore's Workforce with Confidence

At RNS Technology, we believe that the right people drive business success. Established in Singapore, we specialize in providing high-quality, reliable, and Compliance-focused manpower solutions across various industries including Construction, Rail Engineering, and Facility Services.

We go beyond general recruitment. RNS Technologies is a specialized partner for Singapore's Construction and Rail Engineering sectors (Rolling Stock, Signaling, P-Way). We handle the complete operational burden—managing MOM Work Passes, Levies, Payroll, and Dormitory logistics—so you can focus on project execution.

We adhere strictly to Ministry of Manpower (MOM) regulations, ensuring peace of mind for our clients and fair treatment for our staff. Our rigorous screening process ensures that only the most qualified candidates are deployed to your site. All deployed staff are vetted for BCA CoreTrade, CSOC, and BCSS safety compliance.
